# **SIXTY-NINE REASONS**

Instrumental

This track is notable for being added to the album almost *four* years after release.  Commuted's second and third albums started with tonal instrumentals to stirring effect, and fan clamoring caused the band to record a track in shortly after the release of the 1995 megahit disc, *That Look of Release*.  The band has never explained why the instrumental track used the spelt out name of the number versus the album's decimal numbering.
