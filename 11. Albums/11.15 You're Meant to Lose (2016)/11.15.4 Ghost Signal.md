# **GHOST SIGNAL**

---

genre: Grunge/Post-Grunge, slow build, hypnotic verses, explosive chorus, deep layering of guitar feedback and synth interference

Verse 1
Fingernails tapping on a black glass sky,
Static hums where the wind used to sigh.
You left the radio on just to hear a voice,
But all you get is echoes—was there ever a choice?

The streetlight flickers like a dial-up dream,
Fluorescent ghosts swim in the high beam.
You type your name just to see if it’s real,
But the search bar stares back—it’s got nothing to feel.

Pre-Chorus
Is there anybody out there?
Or just reflections in the screen glare?
A thousand friends, but none to call,
Just pixels waiting for the fall.

Chorus
We lost the thread, we lost the touch,
Signals crossed, now nothing’s much.
They built a world without a place,
Now every face is just a face.
You can’t hold light, you can’t hold sound,
And once you’re lost, you don’t get found.

Verse 2
Bare feet step where footprints fade,
The driveway glows like a new cascade.
The porch light buzzes like a dial tone sigh,
You can log in, but you can’t say why.

Your name’s a handle, your heart’s a feed,
They sold connection, but they sold you need.
Your past is data, your lover’s a ping,
And the sky’s too dim for anything.

Pre-Chorus
Is there anybody out there?
Or just reflections in the screen glare?
A thousand lives, but none your own,

Chorus

Bridge
You step inside, but you’re already gone,
A message unsent, a half-written song.
The screen burns white, your hands disappear,
The more you reach, the less you’re here.

Outro
You search for meaning in the feed,
But all you find is memory bleed
