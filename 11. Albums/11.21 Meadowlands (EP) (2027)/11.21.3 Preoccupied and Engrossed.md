[[11.21 Meadowlands (EP) (2027)]]

(verse 1)
Static charged the air, unseen, 
Hollow echoes where dreams had been 
The pulse of dread held tight her heart,  
In silence, she awaited part.  

(verse 2)
Pale moonlight cast upon the floor,  
Adversity—a shrouded score,
The figure loomed—a wraith of night;   
As bindings employed tied their blight.  

(prechorus)
What horror stirs within this guise?
Each thrust an ache that twists and ties,   
The glint of eyes that thirst for pain;   
Her spirit caught within disdain.  

(chorus)
Death’s hymn resounded in her chest,  
Yet had she known this final tune?  
'Twas not just loss—a cruel jest—  
A twisted dance beneath the moon.  

(verse 3)
Ridges pressed against her seething core,
In every plunge she felt before,    
Unwanted pleasure laced with fright;   
Now punctured dreams took flight from sight.  

(verse 4)
A scream erupted—splintering glass,  
From throat that once had sung with might;   
What songs of joy now turned to ash?   
What shadows thrived beyond the light?    

(prechorus)
Gross throes of misbegotten glee,  
Waiting locked in agony’s decree—  
The demon’s breath on fevered skin;    
Each pulse a vice; where hope has been.   

(chorus)
Death’s hymn resounded in her chest,  
Yet had she known this final tune?  
'Twas not just loss—a cruel jest—  
A twisted dance beneath the moon.  

(interlude)
The pain exalted chaos wrought—    
A symphony of lost despair;   
Within this rapture madness caught—    
Men mourn as women learn to stare.   

(verse 5)
Down deep into that brackish sea—
No mirrors left for her to see—        
Fucked under by a tide so cruel;   
Just waves of horror lingered cool.   

(verse 6)
When darkness sings its siren song,   
In hell's embrace it feels so wrong— 
Whose notes shall choke the air they breathe?    
And yet they bind in death’s bequeath.   

(chorus)
Death’s hymn resounded in her chest,  
Yet had she known this final tune?  
'Twas not just loss—a cruel jest—  
A twisted dance beneath the moon.  

(outro)
So here she waits—the blighted thrall— 
Life bleeding out; her tepid pall—    
Entrapped by fury's ghastly kiss;      
And in such sorrow finds no bliss.
