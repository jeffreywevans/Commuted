**\[2009 ESPN COLLEGE GAMEDAY FEATURE — “*Go For Three*” by Commuted\]**  
**Title: “The Rivalry That Eats Its Young”**  
**Narration by Kirk Herbstreit (slightly too emotional) \+ voiceovers by Chris Fowler and Urban Meyer.**

---

**\[OPENING SHOT: Slo-mo cornfield. Fog rising. Faint whistle in the distance.\]**  
**\[Text on screen: November 2009\. Columbus, Ohio.\]**  
**\[Cue song — the opening bass rumble of “Go For Three” begins, low and menacing.\]**

---

🎙️ **HERBSTREIT (VO):**

“In Ohio, we don’t teach our kids nursery rhymes.  We teach them **The Game.**”

**\[Cut to: black-and-white photos of Woody Hayes punching the air. Michigan players kneeling in slush.\]**

🎙️ **CHRIS FOWLER (VO):**

“The hatred isn’t seasonal. It’s inherited.”

**\[Clip: slow-motion of an Ohio State player screaming at the Michigan bench after a sack. Wind. Breath. Frost on the facemask.\]**

🎙️ **URBAN MEYER (VO):**

“You don’t just *beat* Michigan.  You bury them. And then you *go for two.*”

---

**\[CHORUS HITS: “*Go for two / 'Cause you hate their name…*”\]**  
**\[Footage montage: helmet collisions, slow pans of tailgates with dogs in OSU sweaters, angry dads in ponchos.\]**

---

**\[INTERVIEW CLIP — RANDOM FAN, facepaint running in sleet\]**

“I told my wife if we lose, I’m not comin’ home.  I’m just… *not.*”

---

**\[Verse 2 kicks in — “*Scarlet in my bloodstream / Helmet cracked with spite*”\]**  
**\[Slow-motion overhead drone shot of Ohio Stadium packed, swirling snow, players running out of the tunnel.\]**

🎙️ **HERBSTREIT (VO):**

“This song? It’s not about football.  It’s about *remembering why we fight in the first place.*”

---

**\[BRIDGE PLAYS — “*I’d run up the score / On their dreams…*”\]**  
**\[Quick cuts: marching band steps in unison, an OSU linebacker screaming into the sideline camera, fans waving flags violently.\]**

🎙️ **CHRIS FOWLER (VO):**

“Commuted turned the game into a **psalm of rage.**”

🎙️ **URBAN MEYER (VO):**

“It’s not about stats. It’s not about titles.  It’s about **never letting them forget.**”

---

**\[Final Chorus Drops — “*Go for two? Hell, I’d go for more / I’d go for fire / I’d go for war*”\]**  
**\[Montage ends with a slow-motion field goal sailing through snow — the crowd behind it exploding in scarlet and gray.\]**

---

**\[FADE TO BLACK.\]**

**\[Final on-screen quote (white text):\]**

*“Because when I see that M… I don’t just play.*  
*I settle a score.”*

---

🎙️ **FOWLER (VO):**

“Kickoff is noon Eastern.  And Hell is coming with it.”

**\[CUT TO: THE ESPN COLLEGE GAMEDAY LOGO\]**  
**\[Song fades out with a distorted echo of: “Go for three… Go for war…”\]**
