# The Moment

She’s hooked in the first four bars—*what is this?* A woman’s voice that isn’t begging, isn’t apologizing, isn’t smothered in metaphor. The words slide between pop cliché and something so feral it hurts. The band comes in under her—twisted, warbly, *psychedelic and sharp*, and it sounds both like 1982 and like *nothing* she’s heard since last week’s club set at The Limelight.

> Old stories spinning, oh, can’t take a seat.  
> "Are you hard, Jeff?" it slipped from my lips,  
> The thrill in the air, a dangerous trip.*
