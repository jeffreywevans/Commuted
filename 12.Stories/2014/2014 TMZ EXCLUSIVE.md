# 🗞️ TMZ EXCLUSIVE – February 2014

🎤 **“Commuted Implodes Backstage at Grammys—Violence, Gwyneth, and a Very Bad Phone Number”**

They won big… and burned down even bigger.

Alternative symphonic metal juggernauts Commuted took home Song of the Year for “*Snowglobe*” (accepted by John Legend) and Album of the Year for Be First Be Smarter or Cheat (accepted by Avril Lavigne)—but didn’t show up to collect either award.

Why?

Because backstage was a full-blown psychological opera, and TMZ has the exclusive.

🥊 What We Know:

> - Jeremy Gilley showed up with a bandaged arm, reeking of tequila, and allegedly kissed Gwyneth Paltrow mid-hug on the cheek. Security was called. Gwyneth apparently said, “I’ve had worse.”

> - Jeff Evans was seen texting during the telecast. One witness tells TMZ that a bandmate glanced at his phone and saw an incoming call from an 859 area code.
   
“They saw the number and just snapped,” said one backstage worker. “It was like seeing a demon that owed them all blood.”

> - Within seconds, Jeff was punched in the face by Jeremy, and kicked square in the nuts by Jim Brown, the band’s drummer and apparent code enforcer.

“You will never even speak her name,” one witness reportedly heard Jim shout, though nobody knows who he meant.

> - Gilley, meanwhile, went missing during the broadcast, only to resurface in a Papa John’s storeroom, post-coital, still wearing his tuxedo and missing a shoe.

> - Bill Davenport was spotted trying to fix a broken in-ear monitor while screaming into a napkin. One staffer said, “He looked like a man trying to keep an entire church from sinking into the ocean.”
