## **INT. BILL’S PRODUCTION ROOM – LATE FRIDAY NIGHT**

*The air hums with the electric-fried scent of patch cables, cold fries, and existential dread. Penn Station wrappers litter the desk. All five guys—no Kendall tonight—are wedged into rolling chairs, camped around the LCD, where Fox News, Reddit, and Twitter wage information war in adjacent tabs.*

**JEREMY** (feet up on the amp rack, phone in one hand):
Yo, Bill.  Some dude here says he saw you eating a hot dog on Superior’s shore and claim “the water would bless it.”  Fact or fiction?

**BILL** (not looking up, threading a synth cable):
Kielbasa, not hot dog. And I said “consecrate,” which is just science.

**GILLEY** (slouched, grinning, reading off his phone):
Hey, Jer. Listen to this:

> [!attention] Reddit
> “Did Jeff Evans install fluoride mind control in Ghana, or is he just a Midwestern cryptid with a water fetish?”*

Should I respond as “The Real Jeff Evans”? Or like, “Aquaman 937?”

**JIM** (deadpan, eyes flicking between the tabs):
Only if you can spell “hydraulic” right this time.

**JEFF** (quietly):
Please don’t. I’m tired.

> [!important] ONSCREEN
> CNN chyron: “DOJ LEAK—EVANS CLEARED? MEDIA FRENZY.” Cut to Fox: “COOPER CONFUSED—COMMUTED WASN’T.” Anderson Cooper’s face morphs into Kathy’s howl from Detroit 2010, then jump-cuts to a fan-made “water cult” meme.

**JEREMY** (leaning in, keyboard poised):
What if I post as Avril? She’s stuck in Montgomery Inn drive-thru, lost in a pulled pork fugue.

**GILLEY:**
You gotta make it Canadian. Say something about poutine, or “lake law.”

**BILL** (now engaged):
Better: Skyline Chili, just ordering a Pepsi, waiting for the multiverse to implode.

**JIM:**
If you get us banned, I’m restringing your guitars with catgut.

**JEFF** (crooked grin, finally):
See if you can swap concert tickets for water filters. Freaks’ll believe anything tonight.

*Laughter breaks out—nervous, sharp, the relief that comes right before dawn or disaster. The thread pings alive:*

**JEREMY** (cracks knuckles):
How’s this:

> [!info] Twitter (draft)
> “Totally Avril here. Traffic in Cincy is a myth. If I’m not there in ten, Bill has to sing *In the Air Tonight* Garth Brooks style. *Superior is Hungry*. So is my Uber driver.”

Post?

**GILLEY:**
Post it. For science.

*Jeremy hits send. Replies explode—memes, gifs, and a fresh debate over whether Jeff is a CIA plant or just a coney dog in disguise.*

**JIM** (watching, almost at peace):
I hope Avril’s Uber gets hazard pay.

*Doorbell rings, a burst of cold night air. Avril’s voice from the hallway:*

**AVRIL (laughing, breathless):**
Sorry! I brought poutine and questionable judgment. Which disaster do you want first?

**BILL (shouting):**
Bring it all. We’re hosting the Lake Erie Apocalypse over here.

*Laughter again. Relief, for a second. The world’s on fire, but inside this box of patch cables and bad jokes, they’re all still alive, waiting for the sun to come back out.*
