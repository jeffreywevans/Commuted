### **Commuted: The Four Surviving Band Members (2021–2024)**

#### 1. **Bill Davenport (Bill)**
- **Age:** 49 (b. 1972)
- **Role:** Multi-instrumentalist, producer, founding member.
- **Physical:** Perpetual suntan, three-day stubble, long hair (80s rock style), usually in silver & black, compact but strong build.
- **Personality:** Stoic, philosophical (especially Stoicism), innovative, deeply technical, sometimes emotionally walled-off but reliable in crisis.
- **Habits:** Drinks moderately, references philosophical concepts, advocates for technology in music, occasionally intense in studio, easygoing elsewhere.
- **Tensions:** Still has unresolved friction with Jim after the brutal “Be First, Be Smarter, Or Cheat” album sessions—mutual respect, but trust and friendship are bruised.
- **Connections:** Deep working relationship with Jeff; mixed professional/resentful dynamic with Jim; reliable, steady rapport with Jeremy; mutual respect with Cremeans, friendly history with Joe Rogan.

#### 2. **Jeff Evans (Jeff)**
- **Age:** 50 (b. 1971)
- **Role:** Bassist, co-founder, creative and financial backbone.
- **Physical:** Tall, chunky, sharp/expressive face, dark brown hair (often a mess), piercing hazel eyes.
- **Personality:** Intense, deeply introspective, low emotional intelligence, diagnosed autistic (2010), creative driver, loyal to Jeremy, brittle with others.
- **Habits:** Reads military history, collects/organizes sound files obsessively (over 14,000), scribbles lyrics on scraps, never shares unfinished work, sharp observer but often blunt.
- **Tensions:** Still haunted by the death of Kathy (on/off lover) and guilt from past affairs (notably Avril); has mutual respect but frequent head-butting with Cremeans; brotherly, often wordless, deep bond with Jeremy.
- **Connections:** Unified rhythm section with Bill; emotionally guarded with Jim, but respects his steadiness; Jeremy is both “translator” and emotional touchstone.

#### 3. **Jim Brown (Jim)**
- **Age:** 50 (b. 1970)
- **Role:** Drummer, backbone and technical anchor.
- **Physical:** Stocky, average height, ruddy skin, always fidgeting with drumsticks, precise movements.
- **Personality:** Reserved, stoic, emotionally repressed but dry-witted, quietly moral (mild Methodist), “pulse” and “backbone” of the group.
- **Habits:** Early riser, meticulous with gear, collects vinyl, practices rudiments compulsively, prefers analog.
- **Tensions:** No longer friends with Bill after the 2014 album’s fallout—cordial but formal. Holds some guilt over not stepping up sooner after Gilley and Kathy died. Admires Jeff’s success, sometimes envies his luck.
- **Connections:** Strong trust with Jeff on musical decisions; respect and quiet understanding with Jeremy; willing to support Cremeans and the rest, but prefers problem-solving over emoting.

#### 4. **Jeremy Evans (Jeremy)**
- **Age:** 48 (b. 1973)
- **Role:** Guitarist, co-founder, “band’s empathy,” currently newly sober (35 days).
- **Physical:** 6’1”, wiry, balding, three-day stubble, runs 5–10 miles daily, marathon-fit by 2024, practical style, always a Volvo guy.
- **Personality:** Most empathic and socially astute, deeply private about home life, smallest ego in band, often “glue” in tense moments.
- **Current State:** Sober for barely over a month, attends AA daily, very focused on simple routines (“left foot, right foot”), kids away at college, emotionally pared down—lives in the now, avoids drama, does not get swept into band melodrama.
- **Habits:** Lyric notebook in pocket, scribbles between sets, absentmindedly taps guitar when anxious, most fit in the group.
- **Tensions:** Guilt over years of drinking (self-medicated after Gilley’s death), periodic frustration at being seen as “the little brother” to Jeff, but loves his brother deeply.
- **Connections:** Brotherly, nearly telepathic link with Jeff; steady rapport with Bill and Jim; gets along with all, avoids conflict, often steps back from group blow-ups.

___

#### **Interconnections & Current Emotional Map**
- **Bill & Jim:** Mutually respectful, but a burned friendship—civil, but only business.
- **Jeff & Jeremy:** Lifelong, unspoken bond; Jeremy is Jeff’s interpreter, confidant, protector.
- **Jeff & Bill:** Cohesive musical engine, trust in the work, friendship is understated.
- **Jim & Jeff:** Creative trust, musical cohesion, but Jim feels Jeff’s emotional distance.
- **Jeremy & Jim/Bill:** Respectful, both see Jeremy as an essential stabilizer; Jeremy trusts them but doesn’t get entangled.
- **All Four:** Surviving, growing older, dealing with loss and regret—sometimes distant, sometimes intimate, always professional when it matters.
