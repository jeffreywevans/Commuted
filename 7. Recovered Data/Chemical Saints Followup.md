**INT. BILL’S PRODUCTION ROOM – LATE FRIDAY NIGHT**

**The room smells like patch cables, too much coffee, and leftover Penn Station.**

*All five guys are jammed onto mismatched rolling chairs, half on their phones, half staring at a slightly-too-small 32” LCD. The screen is open to r/commuted, CNN’s chyron, and a tweet about Scientology snowblower safety, in three tabs.*

---

**JEREMY (feet up on the amp rack, scrolling):**
Yo, Bill.
Some guy here says he saw you eat a hot dog at Superior’s shore and claim “the water would bless it.”
True or false?

**BILL (not looking up, patching a synth cable):**
I don’t eat hot dogs. That was a kielbasa, and I said “consecrate,” not bless.

---

**GILLEY (slouched, eyes sparkling with chaos):**
Hey, Jer. Check out this thread:
> *“Did Jeff Evans install fluoride mind control in Ghana, or is he just a Midwestern cryptid with a water fetish?”*

You should comment. But, like, *deep cover*. Mess with them.

**JIM (deadpan, watching CNN):**
Or you could do the responsible thing and explain that Jeff only controls the water in Indiana, not all of West Africa.

**JEFF (sighing, quietly amused):**
Please don’t.

---

**(On screen, Fox News is running “Cooper Confused—Commuted Wasn’t,” then hard cuts to a supercut of Anderson Cooper squinting at a map and Kathy belting “Superior is Hungry” at Detroit 2010.)**

---

**JEREMY (leans forward, fingers hovering over keyboard):**
What if I post as *Avril* and say she’s stuck at the drive-thru at Montgomery Inn, lost in a pulled pork fugue state?

**GILLEY (elbows him):**
DO IT.
But make it sound Canadian.
Add something about “poutine” and “great lakes supremacy.”

**BILL (now actually paying attention):**

**JIM:**
If you get us banned from Reddit, I’ll replace all your guitar strings with fishing line.

**JEFF (finally grins):**
Offer to trade concert tickets for water filters. See if the conspiracy people bite.

---

*They all laugh. The thread updates in real time, comments flying. Avril’s texts say she’s two exits away, but traffic’s a standstill behind a guy hauling a pontoon.*

---

**JEREMY (cracking his knuckles):**

> “This is totally Avril. Cincy traffic is a myth. If I’m not there in 10 minutes, Bill has to sing ‘In the Air Tonight’ in a fake Garth Brooks accent. Superior is hungry. And so is my Uber driver.”

**GILLEY:**
Post.
For the content.

*He posts it. The replies explode:

- “Can confirm—Cincy traffic is just three dudes and a coney stand.”
- “Do not anger the lake. It already took my keys.”*

---

**JIM (deadpan, eyes twinkling):**
I hope Avril’s Uber is ready for the reckoning.

---

**(Doorbell rings. Offscreen: Avril’s laugh, exasperated and Canadian as hell.)**

**AVRIL (from hall):**
Sorry, guys! I brought poutine and bad decisions.
Which one do you want first?

**BILL (yelling):**
Whichever one pairs best with Fox News and Midwest internet insanity!

---

*They all break up laughing.
Somewhere, the lake rolls over, unbothered.*

Oh my god, he *flirted*.
