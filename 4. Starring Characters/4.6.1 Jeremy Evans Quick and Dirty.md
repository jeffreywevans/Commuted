Jeremy attends AA meetings monthly, no matter where he is. He is daft about effect pedals, loving to buy, assess, and trade them. Jeremy's ego is the smallest in the band, and he often acts as a stabilizing force.
    
- Template Match: Strong. His layered personality and empathic traits align with the template’s requirements for complexity and relatability.
