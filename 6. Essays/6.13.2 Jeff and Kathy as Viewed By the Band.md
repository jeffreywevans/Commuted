## Their View of [[4.5 KATHY ESPICH character bible|Kathy]] + [[4.3 JEFF EVANS character bible|Jeff]]:
- #### They have seen this pattern for years now.
- #### The orbit.
- #### The collision.
- #### The masterpiece.
- #### The wreckage.
- #### The reconciliation.
- #### In the early 90s, they were exasperated.  They tried warnings, interventions, advice, anger.
#### By 1995, they made a considered decision: "Stay out of it."
#### Let the artists collide.
#### Let the hurricanes crash.
#### Because every time they did… history got written.
#### It is no longer exasperation.  It is resignation — and awe.
#### They don’t like it.  They don’t endorse it.  But they understand: "You don't stop a supernova.  
#### You just hope it doesn't take you with it."
