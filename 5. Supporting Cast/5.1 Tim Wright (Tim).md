# **Tim Wright Character Report**

---

**Name and Basic Information**

- Tim Wright was born on July 1, 1969. During his teens, he lived down the street from the Evans family. Originally a member of the class of 1987, Tim twice failed American Government, a required class, delaying his graduation to 1989. His failure allowed him to become peers with [[4.3 JEFF EVANS character bible|Jeff Evans]], [[5.2. Jeff Cremeans character bible|Jeff Cremeans]], and to a lesser extent, [[4.2 JEREMY GILLEY character bible|Jeremy Gilley]]. After high school, Tim joined the US Navy, transforming from an overweight, aimless teenager into a disciplined and skilled corpsman. His military service, particularly during the Persian Gulf War, profoundly shaped his character and life trajectory. After 9/11, Tim moved to Oslo, Norway, where he began a new chapter of his life.
   
**Physical Description**

- What We Know: During high school, Tim was noticeably overweight, with curly blonde hair. After joining the Navy, he adopted a buzz cut and became the model of fitness, reflecting his disciplined transformation. Post-military, Tim embraced the cyberpunk aesthetic, blending practicality with an edgy, tech-savvy style. In the late 1990s, Tim abused anabolic steroids, leading to a bitter experience with priapism, which he often mocks when visiting the US due to its prescription drug ads. He frequently extols the natural beauty of Norway and finds peace in its stunning landscapes, particularly the Northern Lights.

**Background and History**

- Tim’s early life was marked by aimlessness, boredom, and a lack of direction. His decision to join the Navy shocked everyone and catalyzed a dramatic transformation. As a corpsman, Tim served aboard the USS Iwo Jima during the Gulf War, training relentlessly and preparing for a potential amphibious assault that never materialized. After the war, he volunteered to provide medical care to Iraqi Marsh Arabs, extending his service by eight months. His service exposed him to the horrors of war and left him with PTSD, which he addressed through regular treatment. Post-Navy, Tim leveraged his discipline and benefits to enter the IT field, where he thrived. After 9/11, he moved to Oslo, Norway, seeking a fresh start. In Oslo, Tim opened a vinyl record store.  Tim combined his love of music and technology by teaching himself sound engineering and became a producer for Scandinavian death metal bands. Though not lucrative, these efforts earned him credibility and joy. By 2012, Tim had turned to information brokering, leveraging his IT skills and contacts to gather intelligence for high-paying clients, often trading data instead of fully embracing the Black Hat scene.
   
**Personality and Traits**

- Tim is pragmatic, resourceful, and deeply shaped by his military service. His experiences in the Gulf War instilled a sense of chill and non-aggression, though he retains the discipline and focus he developed in the Navy. His PTSD has made him more empathetic and introspective, and his work in IT reflects his intellectual curiosity and technical prowess. Tim’s love of Norwegian death metal, combined with his IT and sound engineering skills, reveals his passion for blending art and technology. His sarcastic sense of humor and tendency to mock absurdities, like US prescription drug ads, add to his charm.
   
**Relationships**

- Tim’s proximity to the Evans family during his youth fostered a natural connection with Jeff and Jeremy. His professional expertise and reliability have made him a trusted resource for the band, particularly for Cremeans, who values Tim’s discretion and technical skills. Tim enjoys discussing military history with Jeff and sound engineering with Bill, which deepens his ties to the group. His role as an information broker often places him in morally ambiguous situations, but he maintains a sense of loyalty to the band.
   
**Internal Motivations and Goals**

- Tim’s primary motivation is to maintain stability and integrity in the face of chaos. His military service instilled a deep sense of responsibility, and his work in IT allows him to leverage his skills to help others, often in unconventional ways. His move to Norway and embrace of the death metal scene reflect his desire for personal fulfillment and connection to a niche community. As an information broker, Tim balances ethical boundaries with practicality, striving to stay true to his moral compass.
   
**Conflict and Development**

- Tim’s conflicts stem from his PTSD, ethical ambiguity in his hacking activities, and the tension between his disciplined military past and his freer, more rebellious present. His time in Iraq profoundly changed him, giving him a deep aversion to unnecessary aggression and a strong moral compass, even as he bends legal rules. His role as an information broker and occasional Gray Hat hacker adds layers of moral and professional conflict, particularly as he trades in sensitive information. Despite these challenges, his passion for music and connection to the band provide him with a sense of purpose.
   
**Narrative Importance**

- Tim serves as a crucial supporting figure, providing stability, technical expertise, and a unique perspective to the band. His background and skills make him indispensable in moments of crisis, and his presence adds depth and complexity to the narrative. His contributions to sound engineering and his connection to the Scandinavian death metal scene further enrich the story’s texture.
