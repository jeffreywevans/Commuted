# **Creator's Statement – *Commuted***

---

## **Why?**  

[[2.2 Commuted|Commuted]] began as a way to tell wild, imaginative stories about sex, drugs, and rock and roll — to give emotional, historical, and deeply felt context to the original music I was generating using Suno.com. This isn’t just a story. It’s a fully constructed parallel history. A vivid backstory designed to make the music feel like it came from somewhere — from someone. From a band that lived.

## **Why Me?**  

Because no one else is this obsessed. No one else has this kind of imagination, vision, or willingness to build a multi-decade character arc across six timelines with savage continuity and soul-level commitment. I remember what 1989 felt like. I understand the sound of 1993 sweat hitting stage lights. I know what we lost when the culture shifted — and I’m here to rebuild it with reckless, reverent detail.

## **Why Now?**  

Because the tools just got dangerous. AI music models like Suno, LLMs for story refinement, character modeling, and nonlinear timeline tracking — we finally have the machinery to *actually* create the kind of massive, interconnected mythos that once took a studio and 300 writers. The goal isn't to play with AI. The goal is to use AI as a creative exosuit. To go bigger. To go deeper. To make a fictional universe so textured and real that the audience *wants to move in.*

## **What This Is:**  

It’s a fictional rock history that sprawls from 1989 into the 2030s. It’s hundreds of characters. Dozens of albums. Lives ruined and remade. It’s over-the-top lyrics, filthy hot sex scenes, and a savage devotion to artistic integrity. It’s a system built to teach myself — and others — how to wield AI like a flame-thrower: surgically and spectacularly.

## **What It Honors:**  

The lost decade of the American Midwest. The rough boys and strange girls who didn’t fit. The sound of a cassette melting in a hot Citation II. The feeling of a dirty basement show before smartphones killed magic. The idea that music could *save you,* even if only for four minutes.

## **What It Wants:**  

To seduce. To haunt. To hurt in the right ways. And to give people a fictional history so real, they’ll Google the band and wonder why they can’t find tour dates.
