> **Date:** April 4th, 2024

> **Shoot context:** Two hours before the now-infamous blackout set at Bowery Ballroom

### 📸 Left to Right:

**Jeff Evans** — leather jacket over a blue button-down. No glasses here — he’s still running on contacts, pre-*Hearing Clarity*. Dressed by Sarah Malcolm to project authority, but his posture is coiled, restrained. He looks like he knows what’s coming.

**Jeremy Evans** — shaved head, lean and wiry. Standing tall, nearly an inch above his younger brother. The marathon body is emerging, even under clothes. Shirtless, you’d see the dedication. At 51, his charisma is steel-threaded and minimalist.

**Avril Lavigne** — all black everything, styled sharply by Sarah Malcolm. She's standing on a concealed block of wood to match visual height lines. No one notices. Everyone feels her gravity.

**Bill Davenport** — second to right, and far apart by design. Flannel-clad, unmistakably chiseled. His posture is angular, soloist. He's been given a riser to separate him slightly — and the hair is new. No more hair metal nostalgia. It's clipped, modern, and razor-precise.

**Jim Brown** — signature hat, silver-flecked stubble, windbreaker thrown over stagewear after the cold draft from the open studio door. He looks exactly 52 and makes it look like a threat. His stance is casual but not careless.



---

> [!note] **Sarah Malcolm's fingerprints are everywhere.**
> Her quiet aesthetic authority shapes the look — Avril in monochrome, Jeff in strategic layers, Bill isolated but deliberate. The band has never looked more unified, or more aware of the camera's mythology.
